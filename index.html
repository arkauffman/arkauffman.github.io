My name is Alexandra Kauffman and I am currently working on a project with John Solis to amp up Impeccable Look's website. I recently graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive Program where I gained an immense amount of skill and knowledge through three months of intensive learning. This past May, I graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Madison with a bachelor's degree in Communication Arts and a certificate in Computer Science.

I created this site in order to have one common place in which I can share my projects, have a place to continously practice programming by updating my site based on the forever progressing programming world, and to share some insight on what life is like as a web developer.

Thank you for taking this journey with me!
