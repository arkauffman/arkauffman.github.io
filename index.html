My name is Alexandra Kauffman and I am currently a student at General Assembly participating in their Web Development Immersive program in Los Angeles, CA. I graduated this past May from the University of Wisconsin-Madison with a bachelor's degree in Communication Arts and a minor in Computer Science.

I created this site in order to have one common place in which I can share my projects, have a place to continously practice programming by updating my site based on the forever progressing programming world, and to share some insight on what life is like as a web developer.
